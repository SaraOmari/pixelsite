Smart Irrigation Systems

Advanced water management technologies optimizing every drop in our arid climate

🔬 Precision Drip Systems

Coverage: 45% of irrigated farmland

Micro-targeted water delivery directly to root zones using sensor-controlled networks. Achieves 85-95% water efficiency with real-time soil moisture monitoring and automated nutrient injection systems.

🌊 Smart Sprinkler Networks

Coverage: 30% of irrigated farmland

Weather-responsive overhead irrigation systems with variable rate technology. Integrated with meteorological data for optimal timing and coverage patterns, achieving 70-80% efficiency.

🏞️ Modernized Surface Systems

Coverage: 20% of irrigated farmland

Upgraded traditional flood irrigation with laser-leveled fields and controlled flow gates. Transitioning to more efficient methods while maintaining cost-effectiveness for large-scale operations.

🔬 Hydroponic Innovation

Coverage: 5% (Rapidly expanding)

Soilless cultivation systems in controlled environments. Achieving 90-95% water efficiency with precise nutrient delivery and climate control for year-round premium crop production.

Disease Control Matrix

AI-powered disease detection and management systems protecting Jordan's crops

🔬 Real-Time Disease Monitoring

Tomato Late Blight

Pathogen: Phytophthora infestans

Risk Level: Critical in humid conditions

Detection: AI image analysis + IoT sensors

Treatment: Targeted copper applications + resistant varieties

Wheat Stripe Rust

Pathogen: Puccinia striiformis

Risk Level: Moderate, temperature dependent

Detection: Drone surveillance + spectral analysis

Treatment: Precision fungicide application

Olive Knot Disease

Pathogen: Pseudomonas savastanoi

Risk Level: Low with proper management

Detection: Visual inspection + bacterial testing

Treatment: Surgical pruning + copper treatments

Cucumber Downy Mildew

Pathogen: Pseudoperonospora cubensis

Risk Level: High in greenhouse environments

Detection: Environmental monitoring + leaf sampling

Treatment: Climate control + resistant cultivars

Pepper Bacterial Spot

Pathogen: Xanthomonas euvesicatoria

Risk Level: Moderate, weather dependent

Detection: Molecular diagnostics + field scouting

Treatment: Integrated pest management

Grape Powdery Mildew

Pathogen: Erysiphe necator

Risk Level: Low with canopy management

Detection: Weather modeling + visual monitoring

Treatment: Sulfur applications + pruning
